The Challenge

Cardiac monitoring should not stop at the hospital.

Many people experience symptoms outside clinical environments, while access to timely cardiac monitoring can be limited. Traditional ECG testing often depends on location, equipment availability, and scheduled appointments, making continuous observation difficult.

+

Our Solution

Heart-O-Care connects people, devices, and care.

Heart-O-Care combines a portable ECG device, Bluetooth connectivity, a mobile application, desktop software, and future AI-assisted analysis into one connected ecosystem designed to make cardiac monitoring more accessible.

Designed around a simple workflow

From connection to review in three steps.

  1. 1. Connect

    Pair the portable Heart-O-Care ECG device with the mobile application through Bluetooth.

  2. 2. Record ECG

    Use the connected device and mobile application to capture an ECG recording when it is needed.

  3. 3. Review & Share

    Visualize recordings in the desktop software, create reports, and share information with healthcare professionals.
