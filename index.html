Connected Cardiac Care, Anywhere.

Heart-O-Care combines portable ECG hardware, mobile technology, and intelligent software into one connected platform for better cardiac monitoring.

Explore Heart-O-Care Our Journey

Cardiac care should not be limited by location.

A better connection between cardiac monitoring and care.

The problem

Access to cardiac monitoring can be limited, especially when symptoms happen away from a clinic. Waiting for an ECG may delay diagnosis and follow-up.

Patients need a practical way to record cardiac activity on the go, while healthcare providers need a better connection to the information that supports care decisions.

Our solution

Heart-O-Care is being built as a connected cardiac care platform that brings hardware and software into one workflow.

  • A portable ECG device built around ESP32 and AD8232.
  • Bluetooth connectivity to a mobile application.
  • Desktop software for ECG visualization and reporting.
  • A future path to cloud synchronization and AI-powered ECG insights.

Designed around a simple workflow

From connection to review in three steps.

  1. 1. Connect

    Pair the portable Heart-O-Care ECG device with the mobile application through Bluetooth.

  2. 2. Record ECG

    Use the connected device and mobile application to capture an ECG recording when it is needed.

  3. 3. Review & Share

    Visualize recordings in the desktop software, create reports, and share information with healthcare professionals.
