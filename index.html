The care gap

Critical cardiac changes can happen between appointments.

The problem

Fragmented data, delayed follow-up, and limited visibility make it difficult for care teams to identify risk early and support patients continuously.

Our solution

Heart-O-Care turns reliable cardiac monitoring into a shared, actionable view so clinicians can prioritize the right patient at the right time.
