Pocket ECG Device

Portable ECG monitoring designed for everyday accessibility.

Heart-O-Care's Pocket ECG Device combines compact hardware, Bluetooth connectivity, and reliable ECG acquisition to support connected cardiac monitoring.

View Ecosystem Future Specifications

Why It Exists

Cardiac monitoring should be easier to reach.

Built for connected healthcare.

The Pocket ECG Device is being developed to make ECG acquisition more practical beyond traditional clinical environments, with a focus on portability, affordability, and simple connected use.

  • Portable for everyday access to ECG monitoring.
  • Designed with affordability and ease of use in mind.
  • Bluetooth-enabled for connected workflows.
  • Built to support the future of connected healthcare.

Key Features

A compact foundation for connected ECG monitoring.

  • Portable Design

    Compact hardware designed for ECG monitoring beyond a fixed clinical setting.

  • Bluetooth Connectivity

    Wireless communication designed to connect the device with mobile software.

  • Rechargeable

    Planned rechargeable power for practical, repeatable use.

  • Future Cloud Ready

    Designed with future synchronization and connected data workflows in mind.

  • ESP32 Platform

    Built around ESP32 hardware for connected device development.

  • AD8232 ECG Sensor

    Uses the AD8232 ECG front-end for portable signal acquisition.
